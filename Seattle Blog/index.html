Seattle

Seattle, a city on Puget Sound in the Pacific Northwest, is surrounded by water, mountains and evergreen forests, and contains thousands of acres of parkland. Washington State’s largest city, it’s home to a large tech industry, with Microsoft and Amazon headquartered in its metropolitan area. The futuristic Space Needle, a 1962 World’s Fair legacy, is its most iconic landmark.

Seattle is very popular for the variety of great food it has. Some of the top food items in Seattle include:

  • Seafood
  • Pho
  • Cream cheese hot dog, AKA the Seattle Dog
  • Chocolate
  • Teriyaki
  • Hom Bao
  • Coffee

Venues in Seattle

Lumen Field, Home of the Seattle Seahawks and Seattle Sounders

Fun fact: The field has an artificial playing surface and open air design with a roof protecitng 70% of the seats.

T-Mobile Park, Home of the Seattle Mariners

Fun fact: Roof is retractable, so there is no possibility of events being delayed/postponed due to bad weather.

Climate Pledge Arena, home of the Seattle Storm and Seattle Kraken

Fun fact: It aims to be carbon neutral by 2024.

Husky Stadium, home of the Washington Huskies.

Fun fact: Husky stadium holds the record for the loudest game in college football historyL 130 decibels in 1992 against the Nebraska Cornhuskers.
